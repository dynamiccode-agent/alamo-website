About Us

Born in Texas.
Brewed in Brendale.

Built on a Warm Welcome.

Alamo Beer Company began in Texas with a simple belief: great beer is best enjoyed in good company.

That belief has now found a home in Australia. Inspired by Queensland's easy-going lifestyle and strong sense of community, Alamo Beer Co brings its simple roots to Brendale — where hard work is rewarded with a cold beer and a friendly place to unwind.

We're not just a brewery. We're the next choice for your new local.

The Taproom

We operate a 15hL 3-vessel Brewhouse with 2×30hL and 6×15hL Unitanks, producing around 1,000hL of beer per annum — using 20 tonnes of Malted Barley & Wheat, 700kg of Hops and 75kg of Yeast.

Our beers are unfiltered, pasteurised, vegan friendly and brewed mostly from Australian products. The taproom is family friendly, dog friendly and an overall social and welcoming place to visit for any occasion.

There's no hiding our brewing equipment away out of sight — grab a table right next to our fermenters and enjoy a schooner or two.
